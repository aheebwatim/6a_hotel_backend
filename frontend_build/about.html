Welcome to 6A Hotel

A quiet corner of comfort and clarity — in the heart of Arua.

Our Story

Founded in Arua, 6A Hotel emerged from a desire to offer guests a refined, minimal, and modern stay experience. Our design draws inspiration from contemporary African simplicity — elegant lines, quiet luxury, and intuitive comfort.

Mission & Vision

We exist to create seamless stays that feel personal, private, and purposeful. Our vision is to become West Nile’s most trusted modern hotel — known for its speed, service, and simplicity.

Why Arua?

Strategically located in one of Uganda’s growing transit towns, 6A Hotel serves professionals, tourists, and locals seeking a modern space to recharge and reconnect. We're just minutes from key government offices, NGOs, and trade points.

Why Choose Us

  • Fast check-in and flexible reservations
  • Minimal, modern room design
  • Reliable high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Quiet and secure location
  • Friendly team that respects your time

Our Approach to Hospitality

At 6A Hotel, we believe luxury isn’t loud — it’s intentional. We focus on the small things that matter: a fast check-in, silent nights, honest pricing, and clean design. Whether you’re here to rest, work, or reset, we’ve shaped an environment where you can do it all — without friction.

“Modern hospitality means getting out of your way — and giving you space to be.”
